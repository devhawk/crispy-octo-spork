About

Here's a little Geology Jules.

Jules graduated with a Sociology degree from California State University at Northridge in 1999 after working with at risk youth in residential facilities and Department of Children and Family Services and Probation. She became the Science Coordinator at St. Aloysius de Gonzaga in Los Angeles for the Arch Diocese. After marriage, motherhood and becoming a global ready coach for Destination Imagination groups who placed in Engineering and Science as well as Social Service challenges she met her mentor, Bob Jackson and so became Geology Jules! Having been an educational guide for four years, when Bob retired, Jules continued to teach and run science classes both in the field, in classrooms and on line! Now Jules teaches science in several states and soon will add locations in other countries! Jules’ passion is in opening discovery in science and engineering for children everywhere!
